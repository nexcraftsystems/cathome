Why Choose Us for Your Cat’s Grooming?

We believe every cat deserves gentle, stress-free grooming that keeps them healthy, comfortable, and happy.

At Cathome Grooming, our team specializes in cat-only grooming services, ensuring a safe and relaxing experience right at home. From basic cleaning to specialized treatments, we use premium, cat-friendly products designed to improve coat condition and overall well-being. Whether you have a playful kitten, a fluffy long-hair, or a cat with special skin needs, we provide care tailored to their unique requirements.
